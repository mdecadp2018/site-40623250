Assessment12

1. What do the red regions in a section view in Onshape mean?

在 Onshape 的剖面視圖中的紅色區域是什麼意思？

They represent an interference between two parts.

代表兩個部分之間的干擾。

2. How do you measure the distance between two surfaces in Onshape?

如何測量Onshape中兩個表面之間的距離？

Simply select them, and look in the bottom right-hand corner of the graphics screen.

只需選擇它們，然後查看圖形屏幕的右下角。

3. What must each part have before Onshape can report accurate Mass Properties?

在Onshape報告準確的質量屬性之前，每個部件必須具備什麼？

A material (density) assigned.

分配的材料（密度）。

4. What file type should be exported to 3D Print something from Onshape?

應該將哪種文件類型導出到3D打印來自Onshape的東西？

STL

5. Does the Onshape mobile app allow you to edit your design via a phone or tablet, or is it just a “view-only” App? What’s another common use case?

Onshape移動應用程序是否允許您通過手機或平板電腦編輯您的設計，還是只是一個“僅查看”應用程序？ 另一個常見的用例是什麼？

It allows you to view and edit your designs, as well as comment on designs wherever you are.

查看和編輯您的設計，以及隨時隨地評論設計
